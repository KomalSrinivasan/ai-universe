    The most comprehensive collection of AI tools, frameworks, and resources for developers.

    Everything you need to build, deploy, and scale AI applications - carefully curated and verified for 2026.

    What is AI Universe?¶

    AI Universe is a curated collection of 110+ verified open-source AI tools, frameworks, and resources. Every entry is actively maintained, production-ready, and includes real performance metrics.

    Key Features:

    • ✅ Actively maintained tools with recent commits
    • ✅ Production-ready frameworks battle-tested in real applications
    • ✅ 2026 verified with latest technologies
    • ✅ Performance benchmarks and real-world metrics
